About

A first-person game about building factories automated by drones to run a machine that terraforms a tiny planet, with emphasis on the weather. As you terraform, geographic positioning of factories becomes key to avoid different types of destructive weather that can occur.

Coming to PC in 2024. Both single-player and co-op is aimed to be supported.

Design, art, and programming with a custom engine by solo game developer @weatherfused.

Sound by Epic Stock Media.
