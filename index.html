About

Skyformer is a first-person survival and automation game where you build factories run by drones to power a machine that terraforms a planet. Build bases, prepare for storms, and harness the weather along the way! Terraforming allows you to access new areas/resources, and can cause different types of storms that affect regions in different ways.

Coming to PC in 2025. Both single-player and co-op is aimed to be supported.

A few of the main development goals:

  • An automation game that's less about optimization and more casual; similar in feel to a survival crafting game. It's a automation game you can still play after a long day of work.
  • An automation game where the surrounding world and environment ties heavily into the gameplay; you can't just build a factories in a random location.
  • A game that simulates weather and makes it a significant part of the game. As a meteorology enthusiast, I'd like to excite people about weather!

Design, art, and programming with a custom engine by solo dev weatherfused.

Sound by Marty Meinerz.
