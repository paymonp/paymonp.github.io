About

Xenion is a first-person survival and automation game about building factories run by drones to power a machine that terraforms a tiny planet. As you terraform, geographic positioning and protection of factories becomes key to surviving different types of destructive weather that can occur.

A couple of the main goals:

  • A factory-builder that requires less pre-planning and calculations. Instead of building massive optimized factories, the challenge lies more in strategic positioning and survival with smaller material production trees.
  • A game that dynamically simulates weather well. As a meteorology enthusiast, I'd like to inspire people to be interested in weather!

Coming to PC in 2024. Both single-player and co-op is aimed to be supported.

Design, art, and programming with a custom engine by solo developer @weatherfused.
